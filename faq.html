FAQ

What is a game hosting service?

A game hosting service is a platform that provides servers for hosting games online. These servers allow players to connect to each other and play games together in real-time.

How do I know which hosting plan is right for me?

The right hosting plan for you depends on the number of players you want to host and the resources you need for your game. Our different hosting plans are designed to cater to different player counts and resource requirements, so choose the one that best suits your needs.

Do I need any technical skills to use your game hosting service?

No, our game hosting service is designed to be user-friendly, even for non-technical users. Our control panel makes it easy to manage your server settings, install game mods, and configure your game settings.

Can I upgrade or downgrade my hosting plan later on?

Yes, you can upgrade or downgrade your hosting plan at any time. Simply log in to your account and choose the plan you want to switch to. Our system will automatically adjust your server settings and resources accordingly.
